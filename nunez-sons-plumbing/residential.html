Residential Services

Honest plumbing for the place you live.

The same crew that pipes commercial buildings shows up at your home — on time, in English or Spanish, and with the job done clean the first time. Serving Los Angeles and Ventura counties.

How We Can Help

From small fixes to whole-house repipes

No mystery pricing, no scare tactics. We tell you what's wrong, what it takes to fix it right, and when we can be there.

Repipes & Pipe Repair

Old galvanized, pinhole-prone copper, or one section that keeps failing — we repair what's fixable and repipe what isn't. Clean routing, proper supports, and your water back on fast.

Drain Cleaning

Slow tub, gurgling sink, or a main line that backs up every few months. We clear the blockage and tell you honestly whether it was a one-off or a sign of something bigger.

Water Heater Installation & Repair

Tank or tankless, repair or replace. We size the unit to your household, install to code with proper venting and seismic strapping, and haul the old one away.

Fixture Installation

Faucets, toilets, sinks, shower valves, disposals. Supplied by you or by us — installed level, sealed right, and leak-checked before we leave. No drips, no wobble, no mess.

Leak Detection & Repair

A stain on the ceiling, a meter that won't stop spinning, a warm spot on the floor. We find the leak with as little opening-up as possible, fix it properly, and show you exactly what happened.

Emergency Plumbing

Burst pipes and backed-up sewers don't wait for business hours — neither do we. Call any hour, any day of the year. If it can't wait, we're on the way.
