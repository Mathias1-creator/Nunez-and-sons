About Us

Second-generation trade. First-class standards.

Nunez & Sons Plumbing is a new company built on old habits — thirteen years of hands-on work, a family name, and a standard that doesn't move for anybody.

Our Story

Eric Nunez didn't learn this trade from a manual.

He learned it the way most second-generation tradespeople do — standing next to someone who'd already spent years doing it right. Watching first, handing over tools second, and eventually running the job himself. Thirteen years later, that hands-on foundation is the whole reason Nunez & Sons exists.

The company itself is new. The standard behind it isn't. Eric started Nunez & Sons to take on the kind of work he'd spent over a decade preparing for: larger commercial contracts for general contractors, property managers, and business owners across Los Angeles and Ventura counties. Residential work hasn't gone anywhere either — it's where the trade started, and it's still treated with the same care.

Every job — commercial or residential — gets the same thing: a clean, code-compliant install, a straight answer about what it costs and how long it takes, and a plumber who shows up when he says he will.
