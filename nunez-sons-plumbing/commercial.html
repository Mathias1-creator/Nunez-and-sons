Commercial Services

Commercial plumbing that keeps your project on schedule.

From ground-up construction to occupied-building repipes, Nunez & Sons delivers clean, code-compliant plumbing for general contractors, property managers, and business owners across Los Angeles and Ventura counties.

Capabilities

What we take on

Thirteen years on commercial jobs taught us the two things every GC actually wants from a plumbing sub: work that passes inspection the first time, and no surprises on the schedule.

Commercial Contracts & New Construction

Ground-up builds and multi-phase commercial projects, bid straight and delivered on schedule. We work from your plans, coordinate with your other trades, and keep rough-in and top-out moving — with documentation ready at every inspection milestone.

Large-Diameter Piping & Repiping

Supply and waste lines sized for real commercial demand. We install and replace large-diameter systems in active buildings, phasing the work so tenants stay open and shutdowns stay short — and every joint, hanger, and slope goes in to code.

Commercial Drainage Systems

Drainage built for what your building actually produces — restaurants, retail, industrial. Floor drains, grease lines, storm connections, and main runs installed with the fall and cleanout access that prevent call-backs years down the line.

Boiler Installation & Replacement

Boiler swaps and new installs done tight: sized correctly, piped cleanly, and commissioned properly. We handle removal, venting, gas, and controls, then walk the finished system with your engineer or facilities team before we call it done.

Tenant Improvements & Build-Outs

Restaurant, retail, office, and medical TIs. We rough-in to the new floor plan, relocate what's in the way, and finish to spec — used to tight timelines, occupied neighbors, and inspectors who've seen everything.

Code Compliance & Inspections

Work that passes the first time. We keep every install aligned with current California plumbing code, prep the job for inspection, and flag legacy problems before they become change orders — so sign-off never stalls your schedule.
